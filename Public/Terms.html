Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

openworld has no backend. There is no openworld account, no openworld server, and no telemetry. The App runs entirely on your device.

We built openworld because we believe your conversations with AI should be private by default — not monetized, not analyzed, not stored somewhere you don't control.

Your Data

What We Collect — And Don't

  • Conversations — Stored only in your browser's local storage. Never sent to us.
  • API keys — Stored only on your device. Encrypted in local storage.
  • Your name — Used for personalized greetings. Stored locally, never transmitted.
  • Usage analytics — None. We have no analytics, no crash reporting, no telemetry.
  • Cookies — None. We don't set any cookies.

When you send a message in the App, your message is sent directly from your device to the AI provider's API (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic). openworld is not an intermediary — it simply facilitates that direct connection.

Each AI provider has their own privacy policy that governs how they handle your prompts and completions. We strongly encourage you to review them.
